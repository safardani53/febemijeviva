AN entrepreneur has shared how to make thousands of dollars in less than an hour. 

Eric Martin is a jack-of-all-trades entrepreneur in the sub-contracting and real estate sectors.

Eric, known as ericmartinmoney to his TikTok family, is a multiple business owner with loads of experience in general contracting and in concrete.

He typically posts videos explaining how he was able to make enough money on his side hustles to quit his job.

His followers look to him for advice on making making tons of cash with very little work, and sometimes none at all.

Recently, he took to TikTok breaking down how potential hustlers can earn up to $2,500 for just 30 minutes of work.

It’s called general contracting and the best part about it - you don’t need any experience to get started.

The general contracting side hustle

A general contractor (GC) is someone who is in charge of the job site and typically oversees the entire construction project.

They are usually tasked with projects requiring the coordination of multiple stages or tradespeople.

Most read in Money

The GC acts as the go-between between the property owner and everyone else working on the project.

This usually includes materials suppliers, vendors, and tradespeople. 

However, the type of work that Eric suggests is a more specified GC that works with just one part of the project, like concrete.

Getting started

Eric suggests that you reach out to someone in the trade, preferably in concrete.

The concrete business may be easier to get started with as Eric said it’s much easier to quote customers.

Find someone in your town that does concrete, get their number, and tell them you are a general contractor and that you’d like to give them some work.

“They’re gonna say yes because everyone wants more work,” Eric said on TikTok.

Once that is out of the way, you’ve done the hardest part.

The next step is to post on Facebook Marketplace that you do concrete work.

After you get someone to hire you, you go to their house and take photos, videos, dimensions, and any other information you’ll need.

Even if you are just starting out and don’t know exactly what you’re doing, Eric suggests being confident and learning as much as you can.

Once you grab all of that information from your client’s house, send it over to the concrete company you hired and ask for a price.

Whatever they quote you, add a few thousand dollars to it and that will be your profit.

“When he tells you he can do it for $7,500, send the customer a quote for $10,000," Eric said.

"You will make $2,500 and it will only take you 30 minutes of your time,” he said.
