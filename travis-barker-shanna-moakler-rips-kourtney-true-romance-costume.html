SHANNA Moakler ripped into her ex Travis Barker, after the drummer and his new fiance, Kourtney Kardashian, pulled off a couple's costume Shanna says she and Travis originated.

Kourtney and Travis transformed into characters Alabama & Clarence from the rocker's all-time favorite movie, True Romance, for Halloween.

They mimicked several classic poses from scenes in the movie, then shared the snaps online.

Thing is, that's also Sahanna's favorite movie, and she was taken back by her exes recycled costume.

She shared on Instagram:  “It’s like an episode of Punk’d.”

For her part, Shanna, 46, shared a photo of her own, featuring her parents with the caption: “True Romance.”

The snarky shot came after, Kourtney, 42, shared behind-the-scenes snaps from her True Romance photoshoot with Travis, 45, on Instagram. 

The engaged couple was dressed up like Christian Slater and Patricia Arquette's characters in the action-packed movie, True Romance.

Clarence and Alabama were star-crossed lovers on the run from the mob after stealing a suitcase of cocaine.

Kourtney captioned the post with a line from Quentin Tarantino's screenplay: "Amid the chaos of that day, when all I could hear was the thunder of gunshots, and all I could smell was the violence in the air, I look back and am amazed that my thoughts were so clear and true, that three words went through my mind endlessly, repeating themselves like a broken record: you're so cool, you're so cool, you're so cool."

True Romance has had a major influence on both Travis and Shanna, and the former couple even named their 15-year-old daughter Alabama, after Patricia's character. 

In May, Shanna reacted to the news that Travis and Kourtney were watching the 1993 movie together.

She told Us Weekly at the time: “What was weird is that they were posting about a movie [True Romance] that Travis and I basically bonded over. I called him Clarence in the movie. We named our daughter after the character Alabama in the movie.

“I walked down the aisle to ‘You’re So Cool’ from True Romance. Our first dance at our wedding was to the love song from True Romance.”

But the couple divorced in 2008, and Kourtney eventually found herself falling in love with the Blink-182 drummer.

Travis recently proposed to Kourtney on the beach while they were surrounded by thousands of roses and candles.

The two then held a post-engagement party with their families, where Alabama and Landon were also in attendance.
