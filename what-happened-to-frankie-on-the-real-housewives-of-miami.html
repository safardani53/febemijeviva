After an 8 year break, The Real Housewives of Miami are finally back! Peacock has officially released the long-awaited drama-fuelled series, and fans have been waiting eagerly for new episodes.

The new season is set to explore Alexia’s discovery of her late ex-husband’s suspected gay lover, as well as the other latest drama going on in the RHOM cast member’s lives.

With a lot to catch up on over the last few years, the main six RHOM stars are filling viewers in on their latest life stories. But the biggest question for many is, what happened to Alexia’s son Frankie?

What happened to Frankie Rosello?

During the first season, housewife and cast member Alexia Echevarria suffered one of the biggest tragedies to be aired on The Real Housewives franchise in history.

In 2011, her then 13-year-old son, Frankie, was involved in a horrific car accident that changed not only his life but his family and friends too.

Frankie was in the passenger seat of a car when it lost control due to road conditions and rain in the summer of 2011. Following the tragic event, Rosello was in a coma for three months. There were concerns at the time that he might not have made it.

Rosello had suffered severe brain damage from the accident but he stayed strong and fought for his life. Thankfully, he survived and had a smooth recovery.

Where is Alexia’s other son Peter?

Things seemed to be getting back on track for the socialite mom as her son was recovering however, other issues concerning her other son Peter began to arise.

Peter began to get into legal trouble following his brother’s accident. Due to the devastating news, Peter didn’t know how to respond and chose to lash out as a coping mechanism.

Thankfully, this didn’t last long and the family is doing better than ever. Peter managed to get his life back on track and is now a successful real estate agent.

What is Frankie Rosello up to now?

Eight years after the accident, the family are doing well. Frankie graduated high school and opened up his own business, Alexia + Frankie’s Beauty Bar with his mother in Surfside, Miami Beach.

The business is booming and the future looks bright for this young entrepreneur.

His brother Peter is also excelling. He attended the Paris School of Business and once graduated, began selling commercial real estate back home.
