A brief history of Rex Orange County

Rex Orange County was born on May 4, 1998, in Hampshire, England. He attributes his musical influences to well-known English bands such as ABBA, Queen, and Green Day, who pushed him to join the school choir. Alex O’Connor began his musical career with drums and piano, which he learned to play before moving on to guitar at the age of 16. After graduating from The BRIT School for Performing Arts and Technology, O’Connor became a drummer.

In November 2016, the artist uploaded his debut software-recorded album on Soundcloud, catching the attention of English record producer Ben Ash, with whom he subsequently collaborated on hits such as UNO, Best Friend, and Untitled. Apricot Princess, Rex Orange County’s second album, was released the next year. He came to fame the same year with singles like Loving Is Easy and Best Friend. He also appears on Tyler, the Creator’s Flower Boy album in 2017. The artist has since released three studio albums and one mixtape. Who Cares?, his most recent album, was released in March 2022.
