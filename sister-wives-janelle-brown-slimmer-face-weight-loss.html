JANELLE Brown has shown off her noticeable weight loss in a new video while reminiscing about the holidays.

The Sister Wives star shared a new selfie video, cherishing the moments of being a grandmother the night before Christmas Eve.

Janelle, 54, looked overjoyed as she told followers that she was at her daughter Madison's house for the holidays.

Maddie, 28, is one of the six kids Janelle shares with her ex-husband, polygamist Kody Brown, 54.

Along with Maddie's three young kids, Janelle also said that Mykelti, 27, was also there with her three children.

As Janelle was making a few phone calls during some downtime, she said she was also "listening to all the hubbub in the halls."

SUPPLEMENTING THE HOLIDAYS

The close-up video also gave followers a glance at Janelle's noticeably slimmer face as the TLC star has slimmed down over the past year.

Janelle has focused on her diet regimen recently, reaching major success by dropping over 100 pounds.

She is a loyal ambassador for Plexus, which she claims helped her achieve her weight loss goals - even during the holidays.

Her co-star and former sister wife Christine Brown, 51, also swears by the beverage.

Janelle took to Instagram earlier this month to promote the weight loss product while sitting in front of her Christmas tree.

Another part of the clip showed her in the kitchen of her Utah Bachelorette pad, sipping on the diet drink.

Janelle wrote in her caption: "Happy Monday, friends! Do you experience bloating, anxiety, sugar cravings, hormonal imbalances, or inflammation? If so, you might find relief with Plexus!"

While promoting the supplement, Janelle has continued to share her transformation with followers on social media.

An insider said at the time: "Janelle has lost a ton of weight. She does not look the same. She's half the size she was."
