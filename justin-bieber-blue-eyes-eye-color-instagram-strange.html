Justin Bieber’s Blue Eyes — JB’s New Eye Color Looks ‘Strange’

Oh, you guys are still here? Sorry, we just got lost in Justin’s dreamy new peepers!

The Believe singer is always trying out new looks with his hair and body, so we’re not surprised he decided to experiment with his eye color. Justin, 19, shared the picture of himself giving the camera an icy, sexy stare, with the self-deprecating caption, “I look strange with blue eyes.”

While most people actually do look weird with color contacts, we think Justin totally pulls them off. He looks like he was born with blue eyes! And it seems like his fans agree — the pic got more than 400,000 likes and in the first hour alone!

Justin Bieber Reveals How He Deals With Heartbreak In ‘Believe’ Preview

Justin sure has a lot of treats for us today! The Biebs kept his fans happy on Oct. 18 by tweeting out another preview for Believe 3D, in which he explains about how tough it can be to keep smiling onstage when he’s feeling down. Could he be talking about his ex Selena Gomez, 21?

He says in the clip, “I could be going through a big argument, and I’m like, ‘I’m about to go on stage right now.’ And we were yelling…and like then I got to rise up on the toaster.” He adds, “[Sometimes] I’m upset, and I don’t want to have to smile.”

Whoa! That is quite a revelation for the Justin. Considering this documentary was being filmed during his tough breakup with Selena in January, 2013, it’s totally possible that he was speaking about how hard it was to hold his career together while his private life was falling apart.

But don’t worry, fans, Justin says he’ll always put on a smile for you no matter what’s going on in his personal life — and smile paired with those new baby blues is definitely a winning combo!
