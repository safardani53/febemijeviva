Autumn Reeser Baby Born: Actress Welcomes Son Dashiell Ford Warren

Autumn tweeted her exciting news following a successful home birth:

After a swift home birth, Dashiell Ford Warren joined our family at 2:45am this morning. We are SO thrilled!!! http://t.co/Rm47BAqmuK

— Autumn Reeser (@autumnreeser) November 26, 2013

[hl_youtube src=”http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SZDuva3ZLfU/” link=”http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SZDuva3ZLfU/” text=”Autumn Reeser Talks ‘O.C.’ Reunion”]

Her husband, Jesse shared little Dashiell’s birth details on his Instagram page. “Dashiell Ford Warren Born Nov 25th, 2:45am. 8lbs, 8oz. More pics/details to come tomorrow,” Jesse revealed on Instagram, along with the couple’s son’s first photo!

We love he keeps his promise of more photos on the way, because Dashiell is just too cute!

Autumn and Jesse married in 2009, and welcomed their first son, Finn, together on May 10, 2011.

Autumn Reeser: Pregnancy Is ‘So Fleeting, So Beautiful’

Autumn documented her pregnancy on her blog, MoveLifestyle, and she revealed that she just loves being pregnant. “Even when my ankles are swelling and I’m getting kicked in the ribs and looking longingly at my husband’s glass of wine, I love this brief and amazing time in life,” Autumn wrote. “It is so fleeting, so beautiful, and I am grateful that I’ve had the opportunity to make this journey not only once, but twice now.”

We’re so happy that her second bundle of joy is finally here!
