A WOMAN has revealed that she did a mega Savers haul and nabbed a variety of brilliant beauty buys.

Mia Lindy Sully, a brunette make-up fan explained that she nabbed the best fake eyelashes ever from Savers, for only £3.

Not only this, but she also picked up her favourite eyebrow gel and a variety of skincare items that were super cheap too.

The fashionista took to TikTok to show off her beauty haul, as she shared her clip with the caption ‘I love Savers.’

She said: “Hi guys, back with another Savers haul.

“Guys, I literally bought so much today and for like, £60, so I'm gonna show you what I got.”

Mia nabbed a variety of cheap beauty buys, as she explained: “So I picked up my favourite Radox muscle soak, these are the muscle soap [bath salts] and they smell really good too.”

Mia also bought a deodorant, as she added: “Soft and Gentle, smells fabulous.

“I love vanilla, that's why I actually picked it up.”

As well as this, the Savers fan stocked up on a car air freshener, two packs of baby wipes, toothpaste and make-up wipes.

Not only this, but she also made sure to pick up her favourite false eyelashes.

She claimed: “These are literally my favourite lashes ever.

“I actually got my lashes done today but I like to have these in my drawer just in case I'm working, I've got shoots and things and my lashes start to go a little bit sparse.

“These are the best lashes ever and I think they were £3.

“They are really good and the lash band is super seamless.”

Mia also nabbed razor blades, micellar water and some skincare items, as she explained: “Then I also picked up these two new L'Oreal products - now I'm excited to try these.

“So this one here is the re-plumping moisturiser and it's got SPF20 in it, which is really, really good because we all need SBF in our regime.

“And then I also picked up the re-pumping moisturising care for your eyes, so the eye cream.

“The ingredients are hyaluronic acid, caffeine, sodium, and at the back it actually says for ages 25-45. Anything to prevent wrinkles.”

She then showed off another one of her make-up favourites, as she noted: “Then my favourite eyebrow gel, which is the Maybelline Express Brow, this is in the shade clear.

“I have actually been using a brown brow gel from NYX which is really nice, but on nights out I prefer clear.”

Mia then explained: “But, yeah, that is my little Savers haul, hope you enjoyed.

“Just thought I'd show you what I got because you guys love seeing the bits I bought.

“But, yeah, all of that for like, £55 so quite happy.”

Mia’s clip has clearly impressed many, as it has quickly amassed 24,7000 views and 853 likes.

Social media users enjoyed Mia’s clip and many took to the comments and left the heart-eye emojis.
