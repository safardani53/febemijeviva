GOING THE DISTANCE

While Stephanie may have dropped some subtle shade, other GMA colleagues have tried to distance themselves from the alleged scandal.

According to Radar, many of Amy’s colleagues have already turned their backs on the 49-year-old anchor.

“She’s lost most of her friends and support inside the network,” a source told the outlet on Thursday.

The insider claims that staffers on GMA are avoiding Amy’s calls for fear of being associated with her.

“People want her out the door as soon as possible,” the source said. “She’s become the most hated woman in TV news!"

GMA fans previously caught wind of rumors that Amy and TJ’s co-hosts, including Robin Roberts and George Stephanopoulos, are angry with the lovers, who went public with their affair after they were outed in November.

“She’s brought scandal to a show the prides itself as a family program — and she also infuriated Robin Roberts who is the face of the show!” Radar’s insider said about Amy.

Although Amy has taken down her social media accounts amid the scandal, many fans have moved to her estranged husband Andrew Shue’s account to share their support for him instead.

Last month, Amy moved out of the $5.2million marital home she once shared with her former Melrose Place star hubby.

SPEAKING OUT

Meanwhile, 45-year-old TJ’s wife Marilee Fiebig broke her silence on the affair with a statement via her lawyer on Wednesday.

“During the holiday season and in light of the challenging times, Marilee's sole focus has remained on the overall best interest of her nine-year-old daughter,” attorney Stephanie Lehman told DailyMail.com.

“To that end, TJ's lawyer and I have been working together to move their divorce forward privately, expeditiously and as amicably as possible.

“Notwithstanding, we continue to be disappointed by TJ's lack of discretion, respect and sensitivity toward Marilee and the party's daughter,” the lawyer added.
