Kia Michelle Stevens is an American professional wrestler currently signed to Total Nonstop Action Wrestling under the ring name Awesome Kong. She is a five-time Women’s Champion, having won the TNA Knockouts Championship two times and the WWWA World Championship, NWA World Women’s Championship and AWA Superstars of Wrestling World Women’s Championship once.
