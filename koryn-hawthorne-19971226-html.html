Koryn Mattanah Hawthorne is a singer of contemporary American Christian and gospel music. Hawthorne reached the season 8 finals of NBC's The Voice singing competition at the age of 17 as a member of the Pharrell Williams team. After placing fourth on the show, Hawthorne signed to RCA Inspiration, a division of Sony Music dedicated to gospel music recordings. Her debut studio album, Unstoppable, was released on July 13, 2018 and has received numerous award nominations, including two at the Grammy Awards.
