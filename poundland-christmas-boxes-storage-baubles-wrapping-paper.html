Poundland's selling boxes designed to store your Christmas tree, gift paper &amp; baubles in perfect con

May 11, 2024
blog

FESTIVE fans are going wild for boxes from Poundland designed to safely store your Christmas tree, baubles and wrapping paper.

If you get your decorations out every year only to find they’re tangled, broken or ripped, then these containers could be the answer.

An eagle eyed shopper spotted them in Poundland, where they were on sale for £2 each.

The box has enough space for 64 breakable baubles, while the wrapping paper bag can hold rolls up to 75cm long.

Sharing their find to Facebook group Poundland Appreciation Society, hundreds have liked the post – pointing out there’s also one for a Christmas tree.

People have said they could be the answer to protecting their precious baubles and breakable decorations each year.

Studio sells similar items, but their tree and baubles boxes have proved so popular they’ve sold out online, although you can pick up their wrapping paper container online for £2.18.

As Poundland doesn’t sell online, if you want to grab one of theirs you’ll have to head to your nearest store.
