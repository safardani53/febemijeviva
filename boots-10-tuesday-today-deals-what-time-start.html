What is £10 Tuesday?

The Boots £10 Tuesday promotion is the gift that keeps on giving for beauty fans on a budget.

Each week the high street chain offers shoppers a range of skincare, make-up and health items for just £10 each - but only for 24-hours.

A spokesperson for the brand confirmed: "The £10 Tuesday’s usually go live from midnight-2am on the Tuesday."

Most of the deals last the entire 24-hours, ending at 23:59pm, but some do expire sooner or sell out.

The great value deals change every week, but once they are gone, they aren't restocked.
