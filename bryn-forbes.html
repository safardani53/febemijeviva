Bryn Forbes | High School & College Career

High School Career

Even as a high schooler, Forbes was so mature for the game helping his team win at the 2011 and 2012 Class B State Championships.

Though he was not the first choice for basketball teams, according to ESPN, he was listed as the third-best recruit for 2012 at Cleveland State.

Forbes was called the 92nd best shooting guard as per ESPN all over the USA, while in Michigan, he was the 11th best in the state.
