A KYLIE Jenner doppelgänger is shutting down the haters who assume she’s had surgery to look like the reality TV star.

She was born this way.

Besides being a makeup guru, TikToker Taylor Madison (@taylormadisonofficial) has something else in common with Kylie Jenner: her appearance.

The beauty influencer said that while people have compared her to the reality TV mogul since she was a teen, those who meet her as an adult assume she paid for her look.

After one commenter said she “clearly got surgery to look like Kylie,” Tay shut them down.

“Do you really think I have that kind of money?” she began in the clip.

“Trust me I do not.”

With her plump lips, full and defined cheek bones, deep set eyes, and black hair, she bared an uncanny resemblance to Jenner.

“I was born like this, and people always assume I’ve had surgery.

“But what surgery are you implying that I had?”

She said she has the old photos to prove she’s au naturel.

“I’ve been told I looked like her since I was about 14, 15 years old.

“I’ve posted pictures of my childhood self and my teenage self and people still always have to assume things.”

In the caption, she doubled down on her claim, stating she wouldn’t even know where to get that kind of procedure.

“What surgeon or surgery can even make you look like another person lol?”

Viewers supported Tay and complimented her looks.

“That’s her real face. Kylie bought hers,” one quipped.

“Technically, Kylie paid to look like her,” added another.

“Babe, Kylie looks like YOU,” a third echoed.
