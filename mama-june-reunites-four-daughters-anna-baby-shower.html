MAMA June has reunited with all four of her daughters for the first time in SIX YEARS to celebrate Pumpkin's baby shower.

June Shannon, 41, was pictured alongside her girls - including reclusive Anna - at the backyard event and was all smiles as she posed for pictures.

The reality star, who is a recovering drug addict, wore a floral dress for the shower and looked delighted to be back with Anna, 26, Jessica, 24, Lauryn, 21, and Alana, 15, all at the same time.

And despite admitting reuniting with eldest daughter Anna - aka Chickadee - was a "a little weird", they all appeared to look happy.

Speaking at the party, June said: "I haven't seen all the girls together at once because the relationship with Anna has always been difficult - because of my mother.

"I wanted to be in the right frame of mind to be able to deal with Anna as it's always been a touchy subject.

"But seeing her and her daughter Kaitlyn was great. She is still with the same boyfriend and it was a little weird to see them.

"But I did what I call 'roll call' with all the girls being there."

June was just 15 when she gave birth to Anna. Their relationship is complicated, however, and the pair have not got on for years.

In 2015, Anna sued June over money that was reportedly owed from filming Here Comes Honey Boo Boo. They also went on to feud over cash made from royalties on promotional jobs they did together.

Their relationship soured even further when June spiralled into an addiction to drugs.

In 2019, June was arrested on drug charges, including possession of a controlled substance and drug paraphernalia charges in Alabama.

She was arrested with her boyfriend Geno Doak, who was charged with third-degree domestic violence, possession of a controlled substance, and possession of drug paraphernalia.

It is estimated that June spent a staggering $900,000 in a year on drugs.

Despite declaring herself sober for 16 months, it was recently revealed that June had avoided prison time after being charged with possession of crack cocaine alongside Geno.

June has agreed to a list of provisions, including random drug screens and random searches of her car, home and possessions, The Trussville Tribune reported.

The Mama June: Road to Redemption star cannot use alcohol or drugs or visit places where they are used and sold, such as restaurants or bars.

The reality favorite must also remain employed, go to counseling, complete 100 hours of community service and be under court supervision for a year.

Meanwhile, Pumpkin is expecting her second baby with her husband Joshua and invited around 50 people to her baby shower, including her mom and sisters.
