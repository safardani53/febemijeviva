Shiva Rose is an Instagram personality and professional blogger who is best known for sharing her nature-driven and lifestyle content. She runs an online web store where we can find a variety of beauty products.

Early Life, Education

Shiva Rose was born on February 8, 1970, in Santa Monica, California, United States. She belongs to Irish and Persian descent. She was raised by her parents Parviz Gharib-Afshar and Carol June.

Her father, who was an Iranian television personality, currently runs a show on a Persian Broadcasting Company, . Meanwhile, her mother, who was an opera singer, had a television show in Iran.

She attended the University of California, Los Angeles, where she completed her graduation.

Career Trajectory & Success as an Influencer

Rose had an interest in naturalist and activist work since her childhood. She eventually created a blog and shared her journey with others.

Rose has an online web store featuring a variety of her own products which include beauty and lifestyle content.

Just a few years ago, she began her blog , and she says she started it to "get closer to the earth and all its beauty."

The blogger has more than 86,000 fan followers on her Instagram account on which she also endorses her own products.

Her beauty products have been featured in Allure magazine with many successful celebrities like Rihanna and Beyonce Knowles.

Personal Life: Unsuccessful Marriage & Divorce

Shiva Rose, who has reportedly been diagnosed with an autoimmune condition, is currently single but she was previously married to Dylan McDermott.

She tied the knot with the actor, who received a Golden Globe Award for his appearance in , on November 19, 1995.

However, after enjoying a beautiful marital relationship for 14 years, she called it quits with her husband in 2009. The former couple shares two children, daughters Colette Rose McDermott, 23, Charlotte Rose McDermott, 13, together.

Five months after the split, Dylan began dating actress Maggie Q and eventually got engaged. However, things did not go very well between them and they parted ways in late April of 2019.

Shiva Rose certainly earns a lot of money through her blogging career and by selling her products, including Rose Face Oli ($120), Glow Face Balm ($75) and Nectar Body Oli ($85), on her self-titled business website.
