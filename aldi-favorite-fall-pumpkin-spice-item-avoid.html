Im an Aldi super shopper my favorite fall products for as low as $3, but theres a pumpkin

September 07, 2024
blog

AS shoppers get ready for fall, an Aldi superfan revealed which products to purchase, and which one to pass.

Katie Kelly is a lifestyle blogger and foodie known as bitsbitesblog to her TikTok and social media family.

Her website is all about deals - especially when it comes to food.

On TikTok, the Chicagoan typically shares her favorite finds at Aldi and then shows her followers how to prepare them.

She has everything from cocktail recipes to desserts to full home-cooked meals.

This time, Katie took to TikTok and shared her favorite fall finds from Aldi - with the exception of one item.

Below are her must-haves.

1. Pumpkin Sliced Loaf Cake - $3.49

This seasonal item was her first find and is an Aldi Weekly find, only available from September 14 to September 20.

The product will be around for the season but the price may change.

2. Berryhill pumpkin butter - $2.79

The Berryhill brand spread also comes in salted caramel apple for the same price.

Katie loves the pumpkin butter which is concentrated and sweetened pumpkin puree, sort of like apple butter being a form of applesauce.

The butter pairs well with a charcuterie board or sweet bread.

3. Yogurt covered pretzels - $2.99

This fall, the pretzels come in two different flavors: Apple Cinnamon and Pumpkin.

The Apple Cinnamon flavor is her favorite but as it is pumpkin season, that one is definitely worth the try.

The Pumpkin flavor has nutmeg and cinnamon and is reminiscent of pumpkin pie.

4. Snickerdoodle almonds - $5.69

The Southern Grove brand makes the tasty nuts perfect for the upcoming holidays.

The ingredients are super clean as it's only made with almonds, sugar, palm oil, cinnamon, canola oil, and salt.

5. Tate’s pumpkin spice cookies - $4.75

The crispy cookies are a Tate's seasonal favorite as the company sticks to its tradition of being thin and light.

The kosher cookies are stacked with white chocolate chips and pumpkin spice flavors.

6. Priano ravioli - $3.25

The ravioli is another seasonal favorite that comes in Butternut Squash or Pumpkin Sage Ravioli flavor.

The Pumpkin pasta is a blend of slow-roasted pumpkin, five kinds of cheese, and toasted sage.

The butternut squash version is also blended with five kinds of cheese and spices.

The only pass from Katie was the Pumpkin Spice Almonds.

The other fall favorites that she said are a must:

  • Bake Shop Apple Cider Donuts
  • Maple Streusel Breakfast Bread
  • Simply Nature Chicken Sausage
  • Apple Cider Donuts
