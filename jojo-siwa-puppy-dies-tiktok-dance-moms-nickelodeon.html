TV star and musician JoJo Siwa's puppy has died following a tragic accident she said could not have been prevented.

The 19-year-old actress shared the heartbreaking news with her social media followers on Friday.

"(Two) months ago our puppy Tooie (O2) joined our family and he brought the MOST joy, laughter, and messes into our life," Siwa wrote in the caption of a TikTok clip.

The video showed her hugging the dog before having him dance to to the song Boom Boom Pow by the Black Eyed Peas.

"My heart hurts so much to say that this morning he was involved in an accident and is now in puppy heaven," she added.

The celebrity said the pet was "perfect" and that she will miss him dearly.
