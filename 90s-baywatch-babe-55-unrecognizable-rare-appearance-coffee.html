90s Baywatch babe, now 55, looks unrecognizable in rare appearance on coffee run in LA

A FORMER Baywatch star has been spotted on a coffee run in Los Angeles looking completely unrecognizable from her time on the TV show.

Yasmine Bleeth played Caroline Holden on the worldwide-syndicated series from 1993 until 1997.

Now 55, the actress was spotted while out running errands with friends in Los Angeles on Thursday.

In new photos, the TV star made an Amazon return at Whole Foods Market.

She was seen carrying a BurgeonNest dog car seat into the grocery store, before emerging shortly after without the item.

Later, Yasmine and her friend made their way to The Original Farmer's Market, where they stopped at Charlie's Coffee Shop for a cup of joe.

The One Life to Live alum ordered an iced coffee while her friend enjoyed food from the LA hot spot and they spent an hour "catching up", according to an onlooker.

Yasmine looked nearly unrecognizable from her Baywatch days as she was spotted wearing a pink t-shirt and baggy denim overalls.

The former model paired the look with nude Birkenstocks and a white straw bag.

She went makeup-free and her brunette hair was tied back into a messy ponytail.

BAYWATCH BEAUTY

Yasmine retired from her acting career around 20 years ago, shortly after her time on Baywatch came to a close.

The iconic model was launched into fame when she was cast as the glamorous and beautiful Caroline Holden in 1993.

The TV star continued in the role until 1997 and appeared in 72 episodes of the beloved show.

Yasmine quickly became a household name alongside her co-stars Pamela Anderson, David Hasselhoff, Carmen Elektra, Nicole Eggert, and Michael Newman.

The brunette beauty's success led to the release of her own swimwear, and she had two high-profile relationships with Matthew Perry and Don Johnson.

SUBSTANCE ABUSE STRUGGLES

But the former Baywatch star has rarely been seen in public over the last several years.

Yasmine faced struggles in her personal life when her career fell apart due to substance abuse issues.

Studios and directors blacklisted her when she relapsed after leaving a rehab clinic and ended up getting arrested in 2001 when police found cocaine in her car following a crash.

While she was in rehab she met strip club owner Paul Cerrito and the pair tied the knot in August 2002.

The couple now splits their time between LA and Scottsdale, Arizona.

In 2013, Yasmine remarked: "Consciously trying to stay off drugs is now part of my life and always will be."
