Lisa Robin Kelly: ‘That 70s Show’ Actress Dies At 43

Lisa had been struggling for quite some time, TMZ reported. On August 11, her boyfriend reportedly took her to the hospital where she registered a borderline fatal .34 blood alcohol level. Desperate to get better, Lisa checked herself into rehab the very next day.

“She had been fighting demons for a while and finally lost her battle,” Lisa’s agent told the site.

Law enforcement has told TMZ that Lisa’s death is “a mystery,” while there were no signs of trauma, no drugs or alcohol in her room, and no vomit in her airway.

Inside Lisa’s Rocky Past

Lisa has been in and out of rehab and has been arrested four times over the past three years, but was working to get healthy. However, after a recent falling out with her estranged husband, Robert Gilliam, reports claim that she went downhill, leaning on alcohol.

Sources told TMZ that Robert was convicted of domestic battery last month after abusing Lisa, and was sentenced to three years of probation and 35 days in jail.
