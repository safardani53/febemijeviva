Details On Kyle Gibsons Wife, Children, Parents And Sibling

The prominent Baseball pitcher Kyle Gibson is making waves in the sports industry. But there’s more to him than just his career. He is living the dream of a perfect family life.

The baseball player is blessed with a loving wife, four beautiful children, supportive parents, and siblings.

Take a closer look at his family here!

Kyle Gibson’s wife is Elizabeth Straatmann

Born on May 6, 1988, in St. Louis, Missouri, Elizabeth is a Plan Controller at Centene Corporation.

She is also a former gymnast at the University of Missouri. In addition, she studied Nutrition, Fitness, and Psychology at the same university.

Her parents are Randy and Leann Straatmann.

Kyle Gibson’s wife and their kids. (Source: Twitter)

While Elizabeth was a gymnast during college, she faced a tough time with an ACL injury in her freshman year but made an impressive comeback in her sophomore and junior years, reaching her best score of 9.875.

Furthermore, she is a devout Christian and, in a 2020 podcast with her husband, discussed their faith and charity work.

They believe in helping others as a calling from God and aim to pass this tradition to their children and continue their philanthropic efforts through organizations like Big League Impact.

Above all, Elizabeth is also an avid reader.

Kyle Gibson and his wife, Elizabeth Straatmann, have been married for over a decade

Kyle and Elizabeth’s love story started at the University of Missouri, where they were both students.

Although the Phillies initially drafted him, he chose education over a professional baseball career and enrolled at the University of Missouri.

The two didn’t start dating immediately. It took a little matchmaking push from Elizabeth’s roommate.

Kyle and Elizabeth were part of the Fellowship of Christian Athletes (FCA) at the university, which brought them even closer, and romance started during the second semester.

In 2009, Kyle’s baseball career began to take off when he was drafted by the Twins in the first round.

That same year, in December, he proposed to Elizabeth and got married a year later in 2010.

Since then, they’ve not only been partners in marriage but also in their shared commitment to philanthropy.

The lovely couple are passionate about helping others and giving back to their community.

They actively collaborate with various organizations to positively impact those in need.

In 2015, they traveled to Haiti to provide economic aid, education, and spiritual support to underprivileged communities through Bright Hope Ministries.

Even during the challenging times of the COVID-19 pandemic, Kyle managed to raise nearly $1 million to help feed children affected by the crisis.

The duo have been married for over a decade and are going strong.

Meet Kyle Gibson and Elizabeth Straatmann’s children

The talented Baseball pitcher and his wife, Elizabeth, have four kids: three daughters and one son. Their names are Hayden, Mills, Rossilyn, and Novilee.

Their journey into parenthood had a challenging start with the birth of their first child, Hayden, as mentioned in The Baltimore Banner.

The MLB star found himself praying fervently as doctors rushed Elizabeth into the operating room during an emergency C-section.

Hours later, their daughter Hayden was born, and her life started with a struggle for survival.

She had been without sufficient oxygen for about 20 minutes and was resuscitated, placed on a ventilator, and wrapped in a cooling blanket.

Despite the rocky beginning, Hayden overcame the odds, and the Gibsons left the NICU with their newborn daughter after eight days.

Kyle Gibson with his wife and children. (Source: The Baltimore Banner)

Over the next nine years, their family continued to grow, welcoming Mills, Rossilyn, and Novilee into the world.

As a professional baseball player, Kyle’s life follows a strict schedule. But at home, he and Elizabeth have figured out a routine that works for them and their children.

Elizabeth keeps a calendar with his baseball games, and the family sometimes travels to watch him play.

When they’re not at the ballpark, they enjoy family time together, exploring new places, and making memories.

The father of four also stays connected with his kids, talking to them every day through video calls and sending them encouraging messages.

Even though he’s a famous athlete, being a dad is what he loves most.

Kyle Gibson’s parents, siblings, and family details

The much-loved player’s family is all about love and support. His parents, Harold and Sharon Gibson, who reside in Indiana, have been there for him every step of the way.

Harold, who’s a baseball enthusiast, even coached Kyle during his high school years. They couldn’t be prouder of their son and all that he’s achieved.

In addition to his parents, Kyle has an older sister named Holly. She’s quite the softball star and earned a spot at Transylvania University in Lexington, Kentucky, where she continued to shine on the softball field.

The Gibsons go the extra mile to support each other’s sports and endeavors. Sure, like any siblings, Kyle and Holly had their fair share of arguments, but when Holly left for college, Kyle realized just how much he missed her.

He considers her a wonderful role model and a source of guidance during those moments in life when he needed it most.

The Gibson family is all about being there for Kyle. They love and support him in his baseball career and throughout life.

Kyle Gibson’s age and achievements so far

Born on October 23, 1987, in Greenfield, Indiana, Kyle Gibson is 35 years old as of now. His career has reached new heights over the years.

In 2022, the Baltimore Orioles made a significant move by signing him to a one-year, $10 million contract.

With over 1,500 innings pitched in his 10-year MLB career, Kyle brings valuable experience and leadership to the team.

He is known for his six different pitches, which the Orioles believe will elevate their pitching staff’s performance and support their young pitchers’ development.

The 35-year-old is not only a reliable player on the field but also a leader off it. In 2023, he has continued to shine for the Orioles, with a 9-6 record and a 4.68 ERA as of July 25.

In a recent game against the Minnesota Twins, he pitched seven innings and recorded 11 strikeouts, matching his career high.

What drew Kyle to the Orioles was not only their interest in him but also the favorable pitching conditions at Camden Yards, their ballpark.

With a one-year deal and promising prospects, Kyle and the Orioles are set for success in baseball.
