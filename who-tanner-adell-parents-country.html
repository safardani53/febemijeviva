Who is country singer Tanner Adell?

May 24, 2024
blog

TANNER Adell is making a name for herself on the country music scene thanks to her unique style, which is infused with an R&B blend.

Since stepping into the spotlight, she has signed with Columbia Records and is now preparing for the release of her debut mixtape, T.

Who is Tanner Adell?

Tanner Adell is a singer-songwriter from Manhattan Beach, California.

She grew up going to school in SoCal but often spent her summers at her family's ranch in Wyoming surrounded by livestock, which helped her connect to her country roots.

"I’ve been a country fan for as long as I can remember," she explained during an interview with Country Now.

"I was listening to country music before I knew what country music was. When you are little, you don’t think about what a genre of music is."

Some of the first songs she recalls listening to were Keith Urban's Somebody Like You and Shania Twain's Man! I feel Like A Woman.

"I was like five years old when I did that. So, I didn’t really know what country music was until YouTube came out. I was like, ‘Oh, my CD is so scratched up. I can’t even listen to it. So I would type in the lyrics of Somebody Like You, and those came up, and I was like, ‘Okay.’ So, I would play the lyric video over and over again.

"That and Josh Turner’s Would You Go With Me? That’s really where my love for country music started," Tanner added.

Her popularity arguably soared thanks to the success of her TikTok account.

Over the years, she has posted clips of her songs and performances on the social media site to almost 350,000 followers.

Tanner's videos are so popular that they have amassed over 6million likes as of this writing.

Who are Tanner Adell's parents?

Tanner was originally born in Lexington, Kentucky, but her adoptive parents later brought her home to California.

Not much is known about her parents because they stay out of the spotlight, but she often praises them for supporting her career thus far.

"My family are my best friends," Tanner wrote in March 2022.

"They have always believed in me and always pushed me to pursue my interests. Whether I wanted to play an instrument or start singing lessons, my family fully backed my pursuits, especially creative ones."

While Tanner might arguably be the most famous Adell child, she is not the only one.

She also has two older brothers, who were both adopted before her.

Tanner's upcoming mixtape will feature eight songs, including her hits, I Hate Texas and FU-150.
