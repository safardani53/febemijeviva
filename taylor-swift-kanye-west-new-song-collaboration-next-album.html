Taylor Swift & Kanye West — New Song Together In The Works

We’re just days away from the start of Taylor’s 1989 world tour, but it’s never too soon to fantasize about her next album.

1989‘s songs welcomed a bunch of contributors, including Jack Antonoff and Imogen Heap. So could Taylor’s next collaboration be with fellow best-selling artist Kanye?

“We’ve never been in a studio together, but, he’s got a lot of amazing ideas,” Taylor told Entertainment Tonight in response to comments that Kanye made back in Feb. 2015 about doing a duet with her. “He’s very creative and [I] completely respect him as a producer.”

High praise, Taylor! Looks like their infamous 2009 MTV Movie Awards moment has been all but forgotten.

Taylor Swift’s Next Album: ‘1989’ Will Be Hard To Top

Taylor also shared that she’ll be waiting a while to record new music — maybe until after her tour is over — because she wants to take all the time she needs to create another amazing album.

“I’m not making the next record yet, that’s going to take a lot of thinking about what that should be,” she told Entertainment Tonight. “With 1989, I love this album so much that I think it’d be doing a real disservice to try and copy it the next time around. I think it’s going to have to be something very different, creatively.”

Kanye or no Kanye, we expect great things from Taylor and we’re sure that her next record will be a smash no matter what!
