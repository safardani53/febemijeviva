Abigail Breslin Got Married To Her Long-Time Boyfriend Ira Kunyanksy

March 25, 2024
blog

Abigail Breslin, an actress, and singer, recently married Ira Kunyansky. Although the wedding arrangements have not yet been confirmed, Breslin posted a photo of her engagement ring on Instagram with the caption,

“Y’all, yer chick got married.”

Kunyansky also teased the wedding on Instagram with a photo of himself and Breslin. According to the caption,

“I adore my other half @abbienormal9”

The spouse of Abigail Breslin is from Odesa, Ukraine.

Ira Kunyansky, who was born on February 16, 1991, in Los Angeles, California, is Russian, according to his Twitter account. Kunyansky’s educational history is unclear, and he did not finish his secondary studies alongside his graduation, according to sources. He presently works for a travel agency. The net worth of the 31-year-old is reported to be approximately $2 million. Kunyansky rose to prominence after his engagement with Abigail Breslin, and he now has 2,316 Instagram followers and 421 posts. His postings mostly show him with Breslin and his pals. According to the bio:

“I bit into AAPL in 2009. Crypto enthusiast.”

He seems to be a Donald Trump enthusiast since his social media profile is filled with Republican cartoons and images.

Abigail Breslin and Ira Kunyansky’s relationship

Abigail Breslin and Ira Kunyansky have not revealed how or where they met. Their relationship became public when Breslin posted a few images of them on Instagram in 2017. The couple became engaged in February of last year, with Breslin flashing her ring in a photo. The caption stated:

“I was like, ‘obvious.’ #engagedyall,” she says.

Glimpses of the couple’s joyful relationship may be seen on their social media pages. Kunyansky shared some photos on their fourth anniversary in April 2021. Breslin paid homage to Kunyansky on Instagram before their engagement, writing that her life is dull without him. Breslin’s father, who was 78 at the time, died from Covid-19 in February 2021, a year before they were engaged.

The couple has been seen together on the red carpet on several occasions. According to rumors, Kunyansky had no idea Breslin was an actress when they first met. Breslin confirmed as much on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon in 2021, saying,

“When we initially met, he had no clue who I was. When I informed him I was heading to Los Angeles, he said, ‘Oh, for what reason?’ “I said, ‘Well, I’m an actress,’ and he said, ‘Good luck!'”

Abigail will next be seen as Trisha Weir in Miranda’s Victim, a biographical crime thriller film. The release date for Michelle Danner’s film has not yet been announced. Other notable actors in the film include Luke Wilson, Andy Garcia, and Donald Sutherland. Breslin is well-known for her roles in films such as Zombieland, Haunter, Wicked Blood, Magie, Zombieland: Double Tap, Slayers, and others. Abigail has also been featured on television programs such as Hack, NCIS, Grey’s Anatomy, Scream Queens, and many more.
