Who is Dakota Fred Hurt's wife Jennifer Sheets?

August 07, 2024

DAKTOA Fred Hurt, star of Gold Rush: White Water, has died aged 80 after a short cancer battle.

But who is his bereaved wife Jennifer Sheets and do they have any children? Here's everything we know.

Who is Jennifer Sheets?

Jennifer Sheets was the wife of reality star Dakota Fred Hurt before his death.

She appeared on his show, cooking for the mining crews as they attempted to dredge up gold in the creeks of Haines Borough, Alaska.

Jennifer largely kept a low profile before her marriage to Fred and so very little is known about her early life.

When did Jennifer Sheets and Dakota Fred Hurt get married?

The couple married in 2016 in a private ceremony attended only by close friends and family.

They seemed to have built a happy life together and often shared snaps together on social media.

Jennifer was Fred's third wife, after his marriage to an unknown woman broke down and then his second wife, Lorrayne Lier, died in 2015.

Do they have any children?

Both Fred and Jennifer have children and grandchildren from previous relationships.

Fred's son Dustin starred in Gold Rush alongside his dad in all 6 seasons.

Jennifer's children Zack and Kayla also appeared in the beloved show while Kayla's husband, Paul Richardson, has been on it since the beginning.

When did Dakota Fred Hurt die?

Daktoa Fred died on July 11, 2023, the day after his 80th birthday.

He had been diagnosed with brain cancer just four months beforehand.

His final Instagram post showed him enjoying a 4th of July parade.

The announcement of his death, posted to his Facebook page, read: "'Dakota' Fred Hurt passed away this morning surrounded by family.

"He fought courageously in his battle against cancer.

"Fred touched many lives and was loved and supported by many."

The tribute asked readers to consider donating to the Mike Rowe Works Foundation, which trains youngsters in skilled labour through apprenticeships and job opportunities.
