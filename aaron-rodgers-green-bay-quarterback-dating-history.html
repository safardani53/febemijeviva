Aaron Rodgers ex-girlfriends: Who has the NFL star dated?

June 11, 2024

FORMER Green Bay Packers quarterback Aaron Rodgers has dated a host of actresses, models, and even an athlete.

Rodgers is best known for his football skills, but he has dated some A-list stars.

Who has Aaron Rodgers dated?

Jessica Szohr

In 2011, Rodgers was linked to Gossip Girl star Jessica Szohr.

They broke up because her schedule was "crazy for a while" but they reportedly gave love another chance in 2014.

“They just couldn’t stay together in [the first] situation but now they have been spending their free time together and things feel good. They fell right back into it,” a source told Us Weekly at the time.

Olivia Munn

After dating Szohr, Rodgers began dating actress Olivia Munn.

The pair dated from 2014 to 2017.

At the same time, they kicked off their romance, Rodgers became estranged from his family.

Rodger's dad said that “fame can change things” in an interview with The New York Times published in January 2017.

Munn had been accused of being the reason behind estrangement, but she shot down the rumors on Andy Cohen‘s SiriusXM radio show in May 2018.

“On my last day when I was filming The Newsroom, I spent the day in my trailer just encouraging [Aaron] to, like, have an honest conversation with your parents,” Olivia had recalled.

Kelly Rohrbach

In the spring of 2017, Rodgers was spotted out on a date with Sports Illustrated model Kelly Rohrbach.

The pair were spotted strolling around the Westchester Golf Course in Los Angeles, per TMZ.

Rohrback used to play for Georgetown University's golf team.

According to reports, Rohrback used to date Leonardo DiCaprio.

Danica Patrick

In January 2018, NASCAR driver Danica Patrick confirmed she was dating Rodgers.

The two made their debut as a couple at the Daytona 500.

Throughout their two-year relationship, the couple went on lavish vacations and even purchased a $28million Malibu estate together.

By the summer of 2020, a rep for the former Indy Car driver confirmed the two had called it quits.

In September 2020 in an interview with SiriusXM’s Pat McAfee Show, Rodgers said that he had a “new and increased love of life.”

“I’ve made decisions and changes and habits that put me in a lot better head space and there’s just a lot of things that have come together in my life over the last few months that have really been enjoyable and reminded me, given me perspective, on life and in football to view things through the most positive lens I possibly can,” he said.

Shailene Woodley

Shailene Woodley announced that she and Rodgers were engaged on the Jimmy Fallon show in February 2021.

Fans of the football star were surprised to learn that he was engaged to Divergent actress Shailene Woodley.

Shailene confirmed she met the quarterback during the coronavirus pandemic.

“We are engaged. But for us, it’s not new news, you know, so it’s kind of funny," she candidly said.

"Everybody right now is freaking out over it and we’re like, ‘Yeah, we’ve been engaged for a while.’”

She added: “He’s first off just a wonderful, incredible human being, but I never thought I’d be engaged to somebody who threw balls for a living.

“Like I never thought as a little girl, I was like, ‘Yeah, when I grow up, I’m going to marry someone who throws balls, yeah!’ But he’s really just so good at it.”

Woodley says she does not know the future Hall of Famer as "a football guy.”

Their engagement lasted a year before they broke it off in February 2022.

A source told InTouch Weekly,  “While Shailene is supportive of Aaron’s career, he put football first and they were barely spending any time together."

The source added, “She felt he neglected their relationship. Neither of them was happy.”

Blu

Rodgers reportedly started dating someone new two months after breaking off his engagement to actress Shailene Woodley.

Rumors surfaced on June 10, 2022, referring to Rodgers' new lady as a witch, calling her Blu of Earth, something Blu denied on her Instagram.

“Ps… My name is Blu. Not Blu of Earth,” she wrote on her Instagram story, adding, “I do not identify as a witch. Y’all are hilarious.”

However, Blue did not deny the rumors circulating that she and Rodgers were dating.

Mallory Edens

In January 2023, rumors surfaced that Rodgers was dating Mallory Edens, the daughter of Wes Edens, one of the owners of the Milwaukee Bucks.

But at the time, a source told People that their relationship was nothing close to serious.

"It's more than friends, but it's casual. Nothing serious at all. He's not looking to rush into anything. It's low-key for now," they said.

"But it's definitely a very close friendship that is evolving. He's not going to do something as quick as Shailene [Woodley]. Live and learn."

While the two have yet to confirm their relationship, Edens has been spotted at many of Rodgers' games, including his debut with the New York Jets.
