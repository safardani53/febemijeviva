Kimora Lee Simmons: I Lost 25 Pounds &quot;By Not Eating&quot;

July 15, 2024

Well, that's one way to do it.

A slimmed-down Kimora Lee Simmons revealed how she recently lost 25 pounds.

"I shed the fat by not eating," the designer, 35, told PopEater's Rob Shuter. "But I don't like to tell the kids that so they think they shouldn't eat."

The mother of three (two daughters with ex-husband Russell Simmons, and a son with current hubby Djimon Hounsu) went on to slightly amend her comments on her seemingly draconian diet.

"I watch my portions, and I eat leaner proteins and vegetables and stuff like that," she said, noting some days she'll limit caloric intake to fruit, smoothies, or protein bars.
