Incredible photo captures real-life Disney scene as a deer and squirrel meet in the woods

February 29, 2024
blog

THIS is the remarkable moment a photographer stumbled across a "real life Disney scene" in a British forest.

Wildlife snapper Villager Jim was walking through the grounds of Derbyshire's Chatsworth House when he came across a young deer, squirrel and pheasant in a "secret meeting" - just like Bambi and the squirrel talking in the 1942 classic.

The three animals were snapped as they stood between a pair of giant trees this morning.

Jim, who caught the impressive snap, said: "There's something quite magical about it.

"It looks like I've accidentally interrupted a secret meeting between them all. It really looked like they were having a chat and I have disturbed them.

"It's very funny really as they are not three animals you expect to see together.

"The moment I saw the scene in front of me, I immediately thought of Bambi."
