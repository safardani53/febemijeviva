SUPERMARKETS are flooded with stylish looks for women – and now you can bag great gear for the man in your life too.

In time for Father’s Day, Clemmie Fieldsend picks the best.

BEST FOR QUALITY

Marks & Spencer

SWAP denim jackets or blazers for a hybrid shacket.

A modern alternative that dresses up a T-shirt and jeans, it is perfect for throwing on post-BBQ when the evening chill sets in.

M&S prides itself on quality and sustainability, making long-lasting items, with breathable fabrics, which are essential for summer.

  • Shacket, £45, T-shirt, £16, cream jeans, £40

BEST FOR PRICE

Nutmeg

IF your fella likes to keep things timeless and simple, but his basics have seen better days, a trip to Morrisons’ Nutmeg is in order.

Amazing for value, its collections are full of wardrobe classics that don’t age.

Plus, at just £8 for a printed T-shirt with a slogan on the back, to wear with tailored shorts, what’s not to love?

  • Beige T-shirt, £8, black shorts, £14

BEST FOR SUMMER ESSENTIALS

F&F

WHO doesn’t love a floral shirt for summer? Whether you’re on holiday abroad or dressing up for the neighbours’ BBQ, this floral number will work well for any occasion.

All Tesco’s F&F clothing is versatile, like this shirt, and offers bargain items that you can get endless wear out of – and its menswear is no exception.

  • Floral shirt, £16, beige shorts, £18

BEST FOR TRENDS

Tu

IF your man is a one-shirt-for-every-occasion kind of bloke, this style from Tu at Sainsbury’s will do the job.

You can dress it smart with tailored trousers or casual with jeans and shorts. The cheesecloth is an updated version of a classic cotton shirt.

While Tu is brilliant for basics, it has lots of trendy looks that will give any bloke’s wardrobe a facelift.

  • Orange shirt, £16, blue jeans, £22

BEST FOR PREMIUM LOOKS

Asda George

IT looks high-end, but George at Asda’s menswear comes with a purse-friendly price tag.

So whether it’s for Scarborough or Sicily, get your bloke in a knitted polo for some designer Italian style, at just £14.
