The Magicians Elephant Is Set To Released On Netflix

July 20, 2024
blog

The Magician’s Elephant, a new Netflix cartoon feature, is set to premiere on the streaming service on Friday, March 17, 2023, at 3 a.m. ET. The plot revolves around a young child who must complete three apparently difficult jobs in return for a mystical elephant. The vocal ensemble includes Noah Jupe in the main part, as well as countless others in key secondary roles. It is directed by Wendy Rogers and is based on Kate DiCamillo’s novel of the same name.

The Magician’s Elephant, a Netflix original, promises to be magical.

On February 16, 2023, Netflix published the original teaser for The Magician’s Elephant, providing a glimpse into the film’s fantastical universe. The idea is established in the teaser, as Peter frantically searches for his lost sibling. He is then instructed to “follow the magician’s elephant.” Following that, the video portrays Peter’s exciting trip as he encounters various individuals and inquires about the magician’s elephant. Overall, the teaser retains a playful tone that fantasy drama fans will enjoy. Along with the teaser, Netflix released the film’s formal synopsis, which reads:

“Peter is looking for his long-lost sister, and when he meets a fate teller in the market center, he has only one question: is his sister still alive?”

The description continues,

“The response, that he must locate an enigmatic elephant and the sorcerer who will summon it, sends Peter on a perilous voyage to accomplish three apparently impossible jobs that will permanently alter the character of his community and lead him on an adventure of a lifetime.”

Fans can anticipate a friendly, cheerful kids/family story that tackles topics such as exploration, family, and love, among others, based on the description and teaser.

More information on The Magician’s Elephant’s vocal actors and staff

Peter is played by Noah Jupe in the cartoon film. Jupe masterfully depicts his character’s naivety and endearing charisma, and viewers can look forward to an unforgettable performance from the young performer. Aside from The Magician’s Elephant, Noah Jupe has appeared in a number of films and television programs, including Dreamin’ Wild, The Undoing, and No Quick Move, to mention a few.

Benedict Wong, who plays the Trickster opposite Noah Jupe, plays another crucial part. Fans can expect a touching performance from the renowned performer, who sounds similarly amazing in his character. Other noteworthy film and television playing roles for Benedict Wong include Doctor Strange, Marco Polo, Shang-Chi and the Tale of the Ten Rings, and many more. Aside from the aforementioned performers, the film also features a slew of others in crucial minor parts, including:

  • Brian Tyree Henry
  • Mandy Patinkin
  • Miranda Richardson
  • Sian Clifford
  • Pixie Davies

Wendy Rogers directed the film, and Martin Hynes wrote the script. Rogers has worked on visual effects for features such as The Chronicles of Narnia: Prince Caspian, Flushed Away, and Batman & Robin, to mention a few. Remember to view The Magician’s Elephant on March 17, 2023.
