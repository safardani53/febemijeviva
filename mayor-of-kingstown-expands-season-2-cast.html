Mayor Of Kingstown Expands Season 2 Cast

May 23, 2024
blog

Lane Garrison and Gratiela Brancusi have joined the second season of Taylor Sheridan’s Mayor Of Kingstown on Paramount+.

The rough-and-tumble city of Kingstown, Mich. is getting a couple of new residents. Taylor Sheridan's Mayor Of Kingstown has added two new members to its cast. 

SUBSCRIBE: Sign Up For Paramount+

Lane Garrison (Yellowstone) and Gratiela Brancusi (1883) will join Academy Award® nominee Jeremy Renner and Academy Award® winner Dianne Wiest in the upcoming second season of the Paramount+ Original Series. The series is currently shooting in Pittsburgh, Penn. 

Garrison will play Carney, one of the higher-ranking prison guards at Kingstown and an associate of Mike's (Jeremy Renner).

Brancusi is set to play Tatiana, a hostess at the Cheetah Nightclub.

Mayor of Kingstown follows the McLusky family, power brokers in Kingstown, Michigan, where the business of incarceration is the only thriving industry. Tackling themes of systemic racism, corruption, and inequality, the series provides a stark look at their attempt to bring order and justice to a town that has neither.

STREAM: Full Episodes Of Mayor Of Kingstown On Paramount+

Additional cast members include Hugh Dillon, Taylor Handley, Emma Laird, Tobi Bamtefa, Derek Webster, Nishi Munshi, Hamish Allan-Headley, and Aidan Gillen.

Mayor Of Kingstown is part of Taylor Sheridan's growing slate on Paramount+. His other offerings include1883 and forthcoming series Tulsa King, 1923, Lioness, Bass Reeves, and Land Man.

Stay tuned for more information about Mayor Of Kingstown , including a premiere date, on Paramount+.
