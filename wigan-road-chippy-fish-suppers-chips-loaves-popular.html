Customers flock to chippy serving fish suppers wrapped in hollowed-out loaf

March 09, 2024
blog

A CHIPPY is earning a crust by serving fish suppers inside a hollowed-out loaf.

Customers are travelling miles for the dish and demand is so great some have been turned away.

Steak and kidney puddings or pies, or chilli and rice are other fillings in the £6 “loaded loaves”.

They also do “loaded lobby” — a local meat stew with beetroot from just £4.

Wigan Road Chippy in Leigh, Gtr Manchester, has urged fans to check ahead to “avoid disappointment”.

It said: “Polite notice to people that are travelling from afar.
