John Bishop Age, Biography

John Bishop, also known as John Joseph Bishop, was born on November 30, 1966. He was born in the Liverpool suburb of West Derby, England, at Mill Road Hospital. He is British by nationality and British-White by ethnicity. John is the son of Huyton residents Kathleen (née Hackett) and Edward Bishop, a laborer. Eddie, his older brother, is a footballer, and he has two older sisters, Kathy and Carol.

John grew up primarily in the Cheshire towns of Runcorn and Winsford, where he attended Murdishaw West Primary School and Brookvale Academy. He studied English briefly at Newcastle Polytechnic before graduating with a BA in Social Science from Manchester Polytechnic. He later worked as a medical representative for the pharmaceutical company Syntex until 2006, when he left to pursue his comedy career full-time at the age of 40.
