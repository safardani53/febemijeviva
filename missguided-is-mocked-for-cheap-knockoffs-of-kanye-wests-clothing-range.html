Missguided is mocked for 'cheap knockoffs' of Kanye West's clothing range

September 18, 2024
blog

MISSGUIDED has been mocked for its “cheap knockoffs” of Kanye West’s Yeezy clothing range.

The online retailer’s 25-piece basics range is available to buy now, and is priced from £6 for a grey bandeau top and goes up to £22 for an oversized sweatshirt.

Fashion fans claim the budget collection has been inspired by the rapper’s Kim Kardashian-approved line, which retails for hundreds of pounds.

Missguided shared several photos of the new athleisure on social media, and attracted a lot of negative comments from shoppers.

Some of the images were later removed from Instagram.

Customers have mocked the similarities between the two brands – the model appears to be rocking Kim Kardashian’s white platinum hair - as well as quality and fit of the products.

Cami top, £8 and Cycling short, £10, Missguided 

Bandeau top, £5 and Bralet, £10, Missguided 

“When @missguided create Yeezy for the poor. Looks absolutely terrible,” commented one unimpressed shopper, as another said: “Oh, Missguided… Yeezy knock offs? Nice try but this looks so cheap.”

“Missguided knock off Yeezy season is hilarious! How can you get the fit so wrong on a pair of trackies, cycle shorts and a crop top. Ridiculous,” mused another.

“@missguided literally stealing designs from Yeezy Season 6 even the model is styled like Kim K. What an absolute shambles,” wrote an unhappy customer.

The Sun Online has contacted Missguided for comment.
