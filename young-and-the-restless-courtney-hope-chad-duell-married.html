THE Young and the Restless star Courtney Hope and General Hospital's Chad Duell got married in a 'Till Death'-themed wedding in Malibu.

They got engaged back in February this year.

Courtney, 32, and Chad, 34, started dating back in 2016 and got hitched over the weekend in an outside ceremony in front close friends and family.

The pair picked a gothic theme for their special day and were over the moon with their guest's effort.

Courtney - who plays Sally Spectra in the CBS show - took to Instagram earlier today to praise their outfits.

She said: "Thank you to everyone that came out last night to celebrate @duelly and me.

"We love you all so much.

"And thank you guys for committing so hard to our Goth theme! The amount of black nail polish, guy liners, chokers, corsets, and canes were more than we could've asked for."

Courtney looked amazing in a red bridal gown with a long-sleeved lace body, ruffle flared bottom, and matching veil.

She completed the look with a face full of glamorous make-up and her hair styled into some loose waves.

Her new husband Chad rocked an all red suit with black detailing on the front for their nuptials.

A number of famous faces also attended their wedding, including The Bold and the Beautiful's Katherine Kelly and Courtney's co-stars Tracey Bregman and Hunter King.

Katherine uploaded a number of stunning snaps to her Instagram.

In one the actress and The Young and the Restless' Jennifer Gareis posed in front of the Till Death red neon light.

The guests enjoyed their meal on a huge table with black floral covers and matching black and gold chairs.

There were red rose petals which covered the top of the tables and a number of outdoor heaters to keep the guests warm.

They also had an adorable cake with a figure of the pair kissing placed on top.

Speaking about their engagement, Courtney told PEOPLE: “He read me the most beautiful poem, because I love poetry, and I started choking up.
