Kylie Jenner And Anastasia Karanikolaous Collaboration On The Limited Edition Makeup Collection

May 08, 2024
blog

Kylie Jenner’s Kylie Cosmetics teamed with Anastasia Karanikolaou, a content producer, to develop a limited-edition beauty line. The forthcoming collection has bright and lively flashes of blue and pink. The collection consists of five items, one of which is a Lip Kit “Stassie Baby” in a pale pink color. Simultaneously, a gloss duo called “Gonna Get Wasted,” which is a light peachy pink, was debuted.

However, the collection did not stop there, as “Twinning,” a transparent hue with blue and pink glitter, was also launched. Aside from the lips, the collection begins its eye makeup with liquid eyeliner pens in pink “4 Ever Ting” and baby blue “Blue Eyed Babe.” The line will be available on Kylie Cosmetics’ website beginning today, June 13, 2022.

Kylie Jenner and BFF Anastasia Karanikolaou collaborate on the STASSIE x KYLIE beauty line.

In a video posted to Kylie Jenner’s YouTube page, the reality TV star and her long-time BFF Anastasia Karanikolaou touted their partnership STASSIE x KYLIE. The pals are shown trying on the new collection in the video, and Kylie is heard expressing that she is “overjoyed” to be “finally” working with her long-time friend.

All About Anastasia Karanikolaou

Anastasia Karanikolaou, better known as Stassibaby, is a well-known American model and social media celebrity. She has a net worth of more than $2 million and is well-known for her Instagram account as well as her friendship with prominent model and socialite Kylie Jenner, with whom she is planning to cooperate.

The 25-year-old social media star is from Los Angeles, California, and is one of the most successful Instagrammers and YouTubers. Anastasia is rumored to be dating Zane Hijazi, a well-known social media personality, and her sister, Alexia Karanikolaou (also a model). Being friends with Kylie since the age of 11, this partnership has sparked a lot of interest among fans and beauty enthusiasts. The BFFs appeared for a photograph in matching latex bodysuits and elbow-length gloves to announce the cooperation.

Kylie announced the news to followers on Instagram last week, writing:

“AHHHH @staskaranikolaou and I first discussed doing a collaboration a few years ago, and we eventually made it happen. A gloss duo, a customized stassie lipkit, a highlighter in stas’s exact shade, and two playful liquid eyeliner pens are included in our tiny collection. It was an honor to collaborate on this with my closest buddy. I can’t wait for you to get your hands on it on kyliecosmetics.com on Monday, June 13th.”

While this isn’t Kylie’s first time partnering with someone for her beauty line, the originality and fun quotient of the goods presented will undoubtedly pique the interest of followers.
