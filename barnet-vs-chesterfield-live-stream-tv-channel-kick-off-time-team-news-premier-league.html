BARNET continue their fight for survival on Saturday as they welcome Chesterfield to the Hive Stadium.

A win in their last League Two game of the season could be enough to secure their place in the league for next year.

The Bees are just two points behind Morecambe who face play-off hopefuls Coventry.

Chesterfield had their relegation from League Two confirmed after their 2-2 draw with Morecambe on Tuesday April 10.

What time is kick-off?

THIS game takes place on Saturday, May 5. 

The match will be held at the Hive Stadium.

Kick-off is at 3pm.

GEORGE LEGG is hoping to be involved in the last game of the season after coming back from injury.
