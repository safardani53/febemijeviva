A TEENAGE girl "impulsively" put naked pictures of her ex on Instagram - because she feared he had done the same to her.

Marley Wise left her ex feeling "embarrassed and humiliated" when he realised the x-rated snaps were online - after they caught the unwanted attention of male admirers.

The 19-year-old claimed she acted out of impulse when she posted the pictures of her former boyfriend because she thought he had done the same to her.

She has been convicted of revenge porn charges at Teesside Magistrates Court after admitting uploading the naked pictures when her "volatile" nine-month relationship ended.

The court heard how the victim's life had been made a "misery" by Wise's act of revenge.

He said in a statement: "I don’t want anything else to do with her and would be quite happy if I never saw her again."

The court heard how troubled Wise snapped last November after "festering” over nude snaps of herself she had sent to her former partner.

In happier times, the couple had sent naked pictures of each other.

After being arrested, Wise told officers she also a victim of the same crime.

Her lawyer Neil Taylor said: "The reason she did it was because he had posted images of her on the internet.

"She has not tried to hide from this - she accepts what she did was wrong."

But prosecutor Lynne Dalton carried out police checks and claimed there was “no record” of any investigation.

The nude snaps were removed after an hour and Wise doesn't use social media anymore.

Unemployed Wise was ordered to pay £100 compensation to her victim, with the money to coming from benefits she receives.
