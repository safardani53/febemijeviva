VICTORIA and Jacqui Hondrou, better known online as the Hondrou twins, created a name for themselves with their crazy fake tan routine.

Every Thursday the duo apply enough fake tan to give half of England a golden glow.

The pair don't use a normal tanning mitt for their looks, instead they opt for rubber medical style gloves.

As well as saving money by not buying expensive branded mitts, the glove method has so many other benefits.

The twins say that using gloves means the tanning products last longer, since they don't absorb any pricey products.

Using the traditional mitt method can result in super streaky legs and arms, but the girls say that's not an issue when using a glove.

Washing the rubber gloves and using them again next time you want to look like you have just stepped of the plane means the trick can save some cash too.

Demonstrating the hack to their keen viewers, it's clear there's a massive difference between the two methods.
