Jann Simon Wenner is an American magazine tycoon, co-founder and editor of the famous cultural magazine Rolling Stone, and the owner of Men's Journal. Later in his career, Wenner co-founded the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame and founded other publications. As a publisher and media figure, he has faced controversy over the Hall of Fame's eligibility bias, severing ties with gonzo journalist Hunter S.
