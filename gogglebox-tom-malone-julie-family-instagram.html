Gogglebox fans rush to support Tom Malone after wife Julie's update

July 29, 2024
blog

GOGGLEBOX fans have backed one of the shows biggest stars after he was seen surrounded by nurses.

Tom Malone's wife Julie revealed he had been giving blood and platelets, posting a picture of him lying back in a chair.

The Gogglebox dad, renowned for his sharp quips and grouchy nature, grinned for the snap.

He was seen with a KitKat and a drink in front of him and surrounded by staff from the centre with his arm hooked up to a machine.

Tom was also shown with a blood pressure cuff on his arm - which isn't used to monitor him but to maintain a small amount of pressure during the donation.

A full donation is 470ml and will usually take between five and ten minutes to take.

More on the Malones

Julie wrote, referring to the Plymouth Grove Donor Centre in Manchester: "Tom giving platelets and plasma today! @givebloodnhs PGDC."

The post sparked a flood of praise from followers, with one writing: "This is amazing to see and thank you for posting this to bring attention!"

Another said: "Thanks Tom, such a fantastic thing to do. You will help so many people who vitally need these to potentially save their life."

A third wrote: "Amazing gift to give Tom. Well done. And Thank You on behalf of those who will benefit from your donation. Bless ya Mate ❤️❤️."

Most read in TV

Tom appears on the sofa most weeks on Gogglebox with wife Julie, at least one of his children and usually a dog or two.

Vanessa, Shaun and Tom Junior have all appeared while another child, Lee, has not.

The younger Tom quit the show last year saying he didn't want to be paid to sit on his "a**e" and has appeared in several reality shows including The Real Dirty Dancing.

He told fans: "So after 6 and a half years it’s finally time to put down the remote, and to say goodbye to Gogglebox."

The star added: "But new opportunities are knocking on the door and it’s time to explore them.

"I look forward to enjoying the show as a viewer and not having to worry about having said something stupid."
