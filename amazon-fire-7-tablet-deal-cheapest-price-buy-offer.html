Amazon is selling a Fire tablet for just 29.99 in 'lowest price EVER' deal

March 16, 2024
blog

AMAZON has slashed the price of its Fire 7 tablet to just £29.99.

The stellar deal brings the gadget down to its lowest price ever.

Normally this 7-inch tablet would cost £49.99.

But a new Amazon offer shaves 40% (or £20) off the price.

It's now exceptionally good value for money – but it's unclear how long the deal will last.

The gadget is very popular on Amazon, with a 4.5-star rating after over 77,000 reviews.

It's also an official Amazon product, and can be returned within 30 days of receipt.

This tablet has a respectable 7-inch display, and comes with 16GB of storage.

But you can expand that to 512GB of storage with a microSD card.

There's a 1.3GHz quad-core chip on board, paired with 1GB of RAM.

Amazon says you'll get 7 hours of battery life for "reading, surfing the web, watching videos and listening to music".

The tablet supports popular apps including BBC iPlayer, Netflix, Disney+, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok and YouTube.

And it's got built-in Alexa voice controls, so you can even ask it questions.

There's a 2-megapixel camera on the back, as well as a 720p HD selfie camera for video calling.

Part of the reason this particular model is so cheap is that it's a "With Ads" version.

That means you'll see sponsored screensavers on the lockscreen and in sleep mode.

You can get the "Without Ads" version for an extra £10 if you prefer.

Just select "Without Ads" on the product page before adding the tablet to your basket.
