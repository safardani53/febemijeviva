Peri Gilpin Returns As Roz For Paramount+ Original Series Frasier

May 04, 2024
blog

Giplin is set to return as a guest star, reprising her beloved role as Frasier's former radio producer at KACL.

The SEABEE Award-winning duo of Frasier Crane and Roz Doyle are about to reunite!

Peri Gilpin is set to reprise her iconic role and guest star in an episode of the new Paramount+ Original Series, Frasier.

When Frasier (Kelsey Grammer) was living in Seattle, Roz served as the producer of The Dr. Frasier Crane Show on KACL 780AM, where they frequently bickered over their conflicting viewpoints on life.

As the show progressed, the two became close friends and helped each other through difficult times. When audiences last saw Roz, she was promoted to station manager of KACL.

STREAM: Frasier On Paramount+

The new series follows Frasier as he moves back to Boston with new challenges to face, new relationships to forge, and an old dream or two to finally fulfill.

While the cast will primarily consist of new characters, Roz won’t be the only familiar face to appear. Bebe Neuwirth will also guest star as Dr. Lilith Sternin, Frasier’s ex-wife, in one episode of the series. 

READ: Bebe Neuwirth Returns As Lilith For Paramount+ Original Series Frasier

Cast members also include Jack Cutmore-Scott also as Frasier's son, Freddy, Nicholas Lyndhurst as Alan, Toks Olagundoye as Olivia, Jess Salgueiro as Eve, and Anders Keith as David. James Burrows is set to direct the first two episodes. 

Frasier comes from writers Chris Harris (How I Met Your Mother) and Joe Cristalli (Life In Pieces), who Executive Produce with Kelsey Grammer, Tom Russo, and Jordan McMahon.
