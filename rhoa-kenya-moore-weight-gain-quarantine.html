REAL Housewives of Atlanta star Kenya Moore proudly showed off her new body after she gained 25 pounds in quarantine.

The reality TV star and former pageant queen has remained fairly quiet during the pandemic.

Kenya, 49, took to Instagram on Wednesday to flaunt her body in a stunning orange dress.

Along with the picture, she revealed: "183 lbs!!!

"The Quarantine has added 25lbs to my 5’10” frame. I don’t mind if you don’t mind #lovetheskinyourein."

Fans might get to see the Kenya Moore Hair Care mogul flaunting her new figure as she joins the rest of the RHOA ladies in filming the upcoming season.

In fact, she might be feeling a little more relieved as Nene Leakes has yet to confirm whether she'll be back or not.

Kendra's bestie, Cynthia Bailey, recently revealed that they still don't know whether the OG housewife will be back and that she hasn't filmed with her yet.

Cynthia told the Just the Sip podcast of Nene's RHOA peach-holder status: "I don't really have any real answers for you, to be honest.

"So, I can just tell you, I haven't filmed anything with her yet. So, that's that."

She went on of the state of their friendship saying: "NeNe and I are where we are now, but for a long time she was a very, very good friend of mine, who I loved very much, who I still have love for.

"I want her to do what is best for her… She was one of the O.G.s on the show. You know, when you think of Real Housewives of Atlanta, you do think of NeNe."

Cynthia concluded Nene is "great for the show."

"I hope they work it out."

On the podcast Cynthia also discussed filming the new season during the pandemic: "It's been very different, you know, filming during COVID-19.

"We have to get tested every week."

She went on: "Excited to be back at work! We're trying to make this show happen, you know, kudos to Bravo for that.

"It's been very challenging, you know, respecting the COVID rules and regular testing's a huge part of it."

Cynthia shared: "And we're filming more from home because we can't go into certain places and be in big crowds.

"You guys are going to get a very entertaining season 13, but it's going to be a little different."

Cynthia recently had to shut down trolls who told her to lose weight despite her losing it for her upcoming wedding.

When a troll pointed out that she had "gained the COVID-15," the bride-to-be corrected her and said she had actually gained 20.

Last time fans saw Kenya, she was struggling to keep her marriage to Marc Daly alive as he quarantine in New York City and she stayed in Atlanta with their daughter Brooklyn.

Despite their fragile marriage, Kenya revealed back in June that she was ready to have more kids with him.
